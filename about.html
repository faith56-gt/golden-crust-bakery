About Golden Crust Bakery

Our story, our people, our passion.

Our Story

Golden Crust Bakery was founded in 2005 by Margaret and David Kamau in a small kitchen in Westlands, Nairobi. What started as a home baking business — supplying neighbours with fresh loaves every Saturday — has grown into a beloved local institution.

Over 20 years later, our recipes have stayed the same but our family has grown. We now employ 18 local staff, source flour from farmers in Nakuru, and bake over 500 loaves a day.
